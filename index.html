Our Policies

Rules, terms and guidelines for a smooth learning experience

📋 Admission Policy

  • Admission is open throughout the year for all courses offered by IMS Coaching.
  • A free demo class is provided before enrollment to help students decide.
  • Students must fill the admission form with accurate personal and academic details.
  • The institute reserves the right to deny admission based on batch availability.
  • Students under 10 years of age require parent/guardian consent for enrollment.

💰 Fee & Payment Policy

  • Fees are to be paid monthly, on or before the 5th of each month.
  • Accepted payment modes: Cash only.
  • A grace period of 5 days is allowed beyond which a late fee may be applicable.
  • Fees paid are non-refundable once the month has commenced.
  • Fee receipts will be issued for all payments made at the institute.
  • Scholarship discounts are available for economically weaker students — enquire at the front desk.

📅 Attendance Policy

  • A minimum of 75% attendance is required to appear in internal tests.
  • Students absent for more than 3 consecutive days must inform the institute.
  • Regular attendance is strongly encouraged for best academic results.
  • Parents will be informed if attendance falls below the required threshold.

📱 Device & Conduct Policy

  • Mobile phones must be kept on silent mode during class hours.
  • Respectful behaviour towards faculty, staff and fellow students is mandatory.
  • Any form of ragging, bullying or harassment will result in immediate expulsion.
  • Students are expected to maintain cleanliness and discipline within institute premises.
  • Any damage to institute property will be recovered from the student.

📄 Study Material & Content Policy

  • All PDFs, notes and practice sheets shared on this website are for enrolled students only.
  • Redistribution or commercial use of any study material is strictly prohibited.
  • YouTube lectures and Telegram posts are public — please share to help fellow students.
  • Content is regularly updated — always download the latest version from the website.

🔄 Cancellation & Leave Policy

  • If a class is cancelled by the institute due to unforeseen circumstances, a compensatory class will be arranged.
  • Students wishing to take a leave of absence must inform the institute at least 2 days in advance.
  • Extended leaves (more than 15 days) may require re-registration.

🔒 Privacy Policy

IMS Coaching respects the privacy of all students and parents. Personal information collected during admission (name, phone, address) is used solely for academic and communication purposes. We do not share your data with any third party. Photos or videos of students used for promotional purposes will always be taken with prior consent.
